# Overview

Emirates offers multiple check-in options to make the departure process convenient. Depending on the airport and route, passengers may check in online, through the Emirates mobile application, at airport counters, or by using self-service kiosks where available.

Passengers should arrive at the airport with sufficient time to complete baggage drop, security screening, immigration, and boarding procedures.

---

# Online Check-in

Online check-in is available for most Emirates-operated flights.

Key information:

- Opens 48 hours before scheduled departure.
- Closes 90 minutes before departure.
- Available through the Emirates website and mobile application.
- Passengers can download or receive a digital boarding pass where supported.

Online check-in helps reduce waiting time at the airport and allows passengers to manage their booking before arrival. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

# Benefits of Online Check-in

Passengers checking in online may be able to:

- Select or change seats.
- Confirm passenger details.
- Add Emirates Skywards membership information.
- Download or print boarding passes.
- Save time at airport check-in counters.

Availability of certain options depends on fare type, route, and airport.

---

# Airport Check-in

Passengers may also check in at Emirates airport counters.

Airport staff assist with:

- Boarding pass issuance
- Baggage check-in
- Passport verification
- Visa verification where required
- Special travel requests

Passengers travelling with oversized baggage, pets, or special assistance requirements are encouraged to use airport check-in.

---

# Self-Service Check-in

At selected airports, Emirates provides self-service kiosks.

Passengers may use these kiosks to:

- Check in
- Print boarding passes
- Print baggage tags
- Select seats

Availability varies by airport.

---

# Mobile Boarding Pass

Many Emirates airports support mobile boarding passes.

Passengers can:

- Receive the boarding pass by email or SMS.
- Access it through the Emirates App.
- Save it to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet where supported.

Some airports may still require printed boarding passes due to local regulations or operational requirements. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

---

# Required Travel Documents

Passengers should have all required travel documents before check-in.

Depending on the journey, these may include:

- Passport
- Valid visa
- National identity documents where accepted
- Residence permits
- Health or entry documents required by the destination country

Passengers are responsible for ensuring their travel documents remain valid throughout the journey.

---

# Baggage Drop

Passengers who complete online check-in must still drop checked baggage at the designated baggage drop counters.

Bag drop closing times vary depending on the departure airport.

Passengers should arrive early enough to complete baggage drop before the applicable deadline.

---

# Passengers Requiring Airport Check-in

Certain passengers may need to complete check-in at the airport instead of online.

Examples include:

- Unaccompanied minors
- Passengers requiring document verification
- Some codeshare bookings
- Passengers travelling with pets
- Special assistance requests
- Certain visa verification cases

Airport staff will complete any required document checks before issuing boarding passes. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

---

# Airport Arrival Recommendations

To ensure a smooth journey, passengers should:

- Arrive well before departure.
- Complete baggage drop before the deadline.
- Allow enough time for immigration and security screening.
- Proceed to the boarding gate promptly after security clearance.

Gate closing times may vary by airport and destination.

---

# Important Notes

- Online check-in availability depends on the operating airline.
- Codeshare flights may follow different check-in procedures.
- Airport-specific regulations may require additional document verification.
- Boarding passes may not be issued online if travel documents need manual verification.
- Failure to arrive before check-in closes may result in denied boarding. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

# Frequently Asked Questions

## When does online check-in open?

Online check-in generally opens 48 hours before the scheduled departure time.

---

## When does online check-in close?

Online check-in generally closes 90 minutes before departure.

---

## Can I choose my seat during online check-in?

Yes. Seat selection is available for many bookings, subject to fare conditions and seat availability.

---

## Can I check in using the Emirates mobile app?

Yes. Eligible passengers can check in using the Emirates mobile application.

---

## Do I still need to visit the airport if I check in online?

Yes. Passengers with checked baggage must visit the baggage drop counter. All passengers must complete airport security and immigration procedures.

---

## Why didn't I receive my boarding pass online?

Some passengers may need additional document verification at the airport before a boarding pass can be issued.

---

## Can I check in for another passenger?

Passengers travelling on the same booking may generally be checked in together, provided all required travel information is available.

---

## What happens if I miss the check-in deadline?

Passengers arriving after check-in closes may not be accepted for travel and may need to rebook according to their ticket conditions.
