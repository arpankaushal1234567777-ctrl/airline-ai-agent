# Overview

Emirates Skywards is the frequent flyer program operated by Emirates. Members can earn and redeem Skywards Miles when travelling with Emirates and participating partner airlines, as well as through selected hotels, banks, retailers, car rental companies, and lifestyle partners.

Membership is free, and passengers can join online before or after booking eligible flights.

---

# Membership

Passengers can create an Emirates Skywards account through the Emirates website or mobile application.

After registration, each member receives a unique Skywards membership number that can be used when booking flights or accessing member benefits.

Membership allows passengers to:

- Earn Skywards Miles
- Earn Tier Miles on eligible flights
- Redeem rewards
- Track account activity
- Access member-only offers

---

# Membership Tiers

Emirates Skywards offers four membership levels.

## Blue

Blue is the entry-level membership tier.

Benefits may include:

- Earn Skywards Miles
- Redeem rewards
- Member-exclusive offers
- Online account management

---

## Silver

Silver members receive additional travel benefits, such as:

- Priority check-in at many airports
- Bonus Skywards Miles on eligible flights
- Lounge access in selected situations
- Additional baggage allowance on eligible routes

Benefits vary depending on the airport and ticket type.

---

## Gold

Gold members enjoy enhanced travel privileges, including:

- Higher bonus mileage earning
- Priority check-in
- Priority boarding where available
- Lounge access at many Emirates lounges
- Increased baggage allowance
- Priority baggage handling

---

## Platinum

Platinum is the highest membership tier.

Benefits may include:

- Highest bonus mileage earning
- Complimentary lounge access
- Priority services throughout the journey
- Enhanced baggage benefits
- Additional exclusive travel privileges

Specific benefits may change over time.

# Skywards Miles

Skywards Miles are reward miles that can be earned through eligible activities.

Passengers may earn Skywards Miles by:

- Flying with Emirates
- Flying with participating airline partners
- Booking hotels
- Renting cars
- Shopping with participating partners
- Using eligible credit cards
- Promotional campaigns

The number of miles earned depends on fare type, travel class, destination, and partner program rules.

---

# Tier Miles

Tier Miles are different from Skywards Miles.

Tier Miles are used only to:

- Reach higher membership levels
- Maintain membership status

Tier Miles generally cannot be redeemed for rewards.

---

# Redeeming Skywards Miles

Members may redeem Skywards Miles for eligible rewards such as:

- Flight tickets
- Flight upgrades
- Seat selection on eligible fares
- Hotel stays through participating partners
- Car rentals
- Retail and lifestyle rewards
- Experiences offered by participating partners

Reward availability depends on seat inventory and partner participation.

Members are encouraged to monitor their account regularly and redeem miles before expiration.

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is Emirates Skywards free to join?

Yes. Membership is free and available through the Emirates website and mobile application.

---

## What is the difference between Skywards Miles and Tier Miles?

Skywards Miles are reward miles that can be redeemed for eligible rewards. Tier Miles are used to qualify for higher membership levels and generally cannot be redeemed.

---

## Can I use my miles to upgrade my flight?

Yes. Eligible bookings may be upgraded using Skywards Miles, subject to availability and fare conditions.

---

## Do Skywards Miles expire?

Yes. Skywards Miles expire according to the Emirates Skywards program rules.

---

## Can I earn miles on every Emirates flight?

Most eligible Emirates flights earn Skywards Miles, but the amount depends on fare type, booking class, and destination.

---

## Can I earn miles with partner airlines?

Yes. Participating airline partners may allow members to earn or redeem Skywards Miles according to partner-specific rules.

---

## Can someone else use my Skywards Miles?

Certain rewards may be booked for other passengers, subject to Emirates Skywards program terms and conditions.
