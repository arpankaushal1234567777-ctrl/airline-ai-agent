# Overview

Emirates offers complimentary onboard meals on most flights. The meals served vary depending on the travel class, flight duration, route, and aircraft.

Passengers with dietary, religious, or medical requirements may request special meals before departure on eligible flights.

All standard onboard meals served by Emirates are halal certified. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

# Standard Meal Service

Meal service depends on several factors, including:

- Travel class
- Flight duration
- Route
- Time of departure
- Operational requirements

Long-haul flights generally include multiple meal services, while shorter flights may include a light meal or snack.

# Food Allergies

Passengers with food allergies should inform Emirates before travelling.

Important considerations:

- Emirates cannot guarantee an allergen-free cabin.
- Meals are prepared in facilities that handle common allergens.
- Other passengers may bring food onboard.
- Passengers with severe allergies are encouraged to carry required medication and consult their doctor before travel. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Are meals included on Emirates flights?

Yes. Complimentary meals are provided on most Emirates flights, although the menu varies by route, class, and flight duration.

---

## Are all Emirates meals halal?

Yes. Emirates states that all onboard meals are halal certified. :contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}

---

## Can I request a vegetarian meal?

Yes. Vegetarian meal options are available and should generally be requested before departure.

---

## How do I request a special meal?

Passengers can request eligible special meals through Manage Booking or by contacting Emirates before departure.

---

## Can I request a gluten-free meal?

Yes. Gluten-friendly meals are available on eligible flights when requested in advance.

---

## Can I request a vegan meal?

Yes. Vegan meals are available on many Emirates flights when requested before departure.

---

## What if I have a severe food allergy?

Passengers should notify Emirates before travel, carry any prescribed medication, and understand that an allergen-free cabin cannot be guaranteed.

---

## Can I pre-order my meal?

Meal pre-ordering is available for Business Class passengers on selected routes and flights.
