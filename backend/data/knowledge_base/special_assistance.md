# Overview

Emirates provides special assistance services for passengers with medical conditions, reduced mobility, disabilities, elderly passengers, pregnant travellers, children travelling alone, and families travelling with infants.

Passengers requiring special assistance are encouraged to notify Emirates as early as possible before departure to ensure appropriate arrangements can be made.

---

# Wheelchair Assistance

Passengers who require mobility assistance may request wheelchair services.

Wheelchair assistance may include:

- Assistance from check-in to the boarding gate
- Boarding and deboarding support
- Assistance during flight connections
- Assistance upon arrival

Passengers are encouraged to request wheelchair assistance before travelling.

---

# Passengers with Reduced Mobility

Passengers with reduced mobility may travel on Emirates flights.

Depending on individual requirements, Emirates may provide:

- Boarding assistance
- Priority boarding
- Airport mobility support
- Assistance during transfers

Certain medical conditions may require medical clearance before travel.

---

# Medical Equipment

Passengers may generally travel with medically necessary equipment.

Examples include:

- CPAP machines
- Portable oxygen concentrators (approved devices)
- Blood glucose monitors
- Insulin supplies
- Medical monitoring devices

Passengers should verify equipment approval before departure.

---

# Medicines

Passengers should carry essential medicines in cabin baggage.

Recommendations include:

- Carry medicines in original packaging where possible.
- Carry prescriptions or medical certificates when appropriate.
- Keep medicines easily accessible during the flight.

Certain medicines may require additional documentation depending on destination regulations.

---

# Pregnancy

Pregnant passengers may travel safely in many circumstances.

However:

- Medical certificates may be required after certain stages of pregnancy.
- Additional restrictions may apply for multiple pregnancies or medical complications.
- Fitness to travel remains subject to airline medical guidelines.

Passengers should review Emirates pregnancy travel requirements before departure.

---

# Travelling with Infants

Families travelling with infants may receive additional assistance.

Services may include:

- Priority boarding
- Baby bassinets on eligible aircraft
- Baby meals
- Assistance from cabin crew where appropriate

Bassinet availability depends on aircraft type and advance reservation.

---

# Travelling with Children

Children travelling with parents generally receive standard assistance throughout the journey.

Parents are encouraged to:

- Carry essential baby supplies
- Arrive early at the airport
- Keep travel documents easily accessible

---

# Unaccompanied Minors

Emirates offers an Unaccompanied Minor service for eligible children travelling alone.

The service generally includes:

- Airport supervision
- Boarding assistance
- In-flight monitoring
- Arrival assistance

Eligibility requirements and age restrictions apply.

---

# Elderly Passengers

Elderly passengers may request assistance when travelling.

Available services may include:

- Wheelchair support
- Airport assistance
- Boarding assistance
- Transfer assistance

Requests should preferably be made before travel.

---

# Passengers with Disabilities

Passengers with disabilities are welcome to travel with Emirates.

Available assistance may include:

- Boarding assistance
- Airport guidance
- Accessible seating where available
- Mobility assistance

Passengers should communicate any specific requirements before departure.

---

# Service Animals

Acceptance of service animals depends on:

- Destination regulations
- Applicable airline policies
- Required documentation

Passengers travelling with service animals should contact Emirates before booking or travelling.

---

# Important Notes

- Special assistance should be requested as early as possible.
- Some services require advance notice.
- Medical clearance may be required for certain conditions.
- Local airport facilities may vary.
- Destination countries may have additional health or accessibility requirements.

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I request a wheelchair?

Yes. Wheelchair assistance can generally be requested before your journey.

---

## How do I request wheelchair assistance?

Passengers should contact Emirates or request assistance during booking or through Manage Booking.

---

## Can I travel while pregnant?

Yes, many pregnant passengers may travel safely. Medical certificates or restrictions may apply depending on the stage of pregnancy.

---

## Can I carry insulin?

Yes. Insulin and other essential medicines should generally be carried in cabin baggage.

---

## Can I carry my medicines?

Yes. Essential medicines are generally permitted. Carry prescriptions when appropriate.

---

## Can I travel with a CPAP machine?

Approved CPAP machines may generally be carried. Passengers should verify airline requirements before departure.

---

## Can I travel with oxygen equipment?

Certain approved oxygen equipment may be accepted subject to airline policies and advance approval.

---

## Can my child travel alone?

Yes. Emirates offers an Unaccompanied Minor service for eligible children.

---

## Can I request priority boarding?

Passengers requiring special assistance are generally eligible for priority boarding where appropriate.

---

## Can elderly passengers request assistance?

Yes. Airport and boarding assistance may be requested.

---

## Can passengers with disabilities travel on Emirates?

Yes. Emirates provides various accessibility services for eligible passengers.

---

## Can I travel with a service dog?

Acceptance depends on destination regulations and airline policies. Passengers should contact Emirates before travel.

---

## Will cabin crew help me during the flight?

Cabin crew provide reasonable assistance during the flight, although certain personal care tasks remain the responsibility of the passenger or accompanying caregiver.

---

## When should I request special assistance?

Passengers should request assistance as early as possible before departure to allow Emirates to make the necessary arrangements.
