# Overview

Passengers may cancel their booking before departure, subject to the fare conditions of their ticket. Cancellation eligibility, charges, and available options depend on the ticket type, destination, booking channel, and applicable regulations.

Cancelling a booking is different from requesting a refund. A cancelled booking may or may not qualify for a monetary refund depending on the ticket's fare rules.

---

# Ways to Cancel a Booking

Passengers may cancel their booking through one of the following methods:

- Emirates website
- Manage Booking portal
- Emirates Customer Service
- Emirates ticket office
- Travel agency (if the ticket was purchased through an agent)

If the booking was made through a travel agency or another airline, cancellation requests should normally be handled by the original booking provider.

---

# Fare Conditions

Every Emirates ticket is issued with specific fare conditions.

Fare conditions determine:

- Whether cancellation is permitted
- Applicable cancellation fees
- Refund eligibility
- Change options
- Travel credit eligibility

Passengers should review their fare conditions before cancelling a reservation.

---

# Cancellation Before Departure

Passengers who cancel before the scheduled departure time generally have more options than passengers who do not.

Depending on the fare type, cancellation before departure may result in:

- Full refund
- Partial refund
- Travel credit
- Cancellation fee
- Non-refundable ticket

---

# No-Show Policy

A passenger is considered a no-show if they fail to travel without cancelling or changing their booking before departure.

Consequences may include:

- Additional penalties
- Loss of ticket value for certain fare types
- Cancellation of remaining flight segments
- Reduced refund eligibility

# Cancellation by Emirates

If Emirates cancels a flight or makes a significant schedule change, eligible passengers may receive one or more of the following options:

- Rebooking on another Emirates flight
- Alternative travel arrangements where available
- Travel credit
- Refund according to applicable regulations and fare conditions

Available assistance depends on the route, local regulations, and the circumstances of the cancellation. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

---

# Travel Credit

In certain situations, passengers may choose to retain the value of their unused ticket as future travel credit instead of requesting an immediate refund.

Availability depends on:

- Fare rules
- Promotional offers
- Applicable travel policies

---

# Special Circumstances

Special consideration may apply in situations such as:

- Serious illness
- Death of a passenger or immediate family member
- Government travel restrictions
- Natural disasters
- Other extraordinary events

Supporting documentation may be required before exceptions are considered.

---

# Important Notes

- Cancellation fees vary according to fare conditions.
- Promotional fares generally have stricter cancellation rules.
- Additional services such as seats, lounge access, or extra baggage may have separate cancellation conditions.
- Codeshare flights may follow different airline policies.

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I cancel my booking online?

Yes. Passengers who booked directly with Emirates can usually manage eligible bookings through the Emirates website or the Manage Booking section.

---

## Is every ticket refundable after cancellation?

No. Refund eligibility depends entirely on the fare conditions associated with the ticket.

---

## What happens if I miss my flight?

Missing a flight without cancelling beforehand may result in no-show penalties and reduced refund eligibility.

---

## Can I cancel only one passenger in a booking?

Depending on the booking type and fare conditions, individual passengers may be cancelled separately. Some bookings may require additional assistance.

---

## What happens to my return flight if I miss my outbound flight?

In some situations, the remaining itinerary may be affected. Passengers should contact Emirates immediately if they miss a scheduled flight.

---

## Can I cancel a booking made through a travel agent?

Yes, but cancellation requests should generally be handled through the original travel agency that issued the ticket.

---

## Can Emirates cancel my booking?

Yes. Bookings may be cancelled because of operational reasons, schedule changes, regulatory requirements, safety concerns, or other circumstances. Eligible passengers may receive rebooking or refund options according to applicable policies.
