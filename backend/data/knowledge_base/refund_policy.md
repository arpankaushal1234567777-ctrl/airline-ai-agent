# Overview

Refund eligibility depends on the fare type, ticket conditions, route, and the reason for cancellation. Some tickets are fully refundable, some are partially refundable after applicable charges, and others may be non-refundable.

Passengers should review the fare conditions associated with their booking before requesting a refund.

---

# Refund Eligibility

Refund availability depends on:

- Ticket fare conditions
- Whether the ticket has been used
- Time of cancellation
- Booking channel
- Applicable government regulations

Refund requests are processed according to the fare rules that applied when the ticket was purchased.

---

# Refundable Tickets

Passengers holding refundable tickets may be eligible to receive:

- Full refund
- Partial refund after applicable cancellation charges
- Refund of unused flight segments

The refundable amount depends on the fare conditions.

---

# Non-Refundable Tickets

Some promotional or discounted fares are non-refundable.

For these tickets:

- Base fare may not be refunded.
- Unused government taxes and airport charges may still be refundable where applicable.
- Fare conditions determine the final refund eligibility.

---

# Refund Request Process

Passengers who booked directly with Emirates may request a refund through the official refund request process.

Passengers who booked through:

- Travel agencies
- Online travel agencies
- Partner airlines

should contact the original booking provider for refund assistance.

---

# Refund Method

Approved refunds are generally returned using the original payment method.

Examples include:

- Credit card
- Debit card
- Bank transfer (where applicable)
- Other approved payment methods

---

# Refund Processing Time

Refund processing times vary depending on:

- Payment method
- Country of purchase
- Banking institution
- Fare conditions

# Taxes and Fees

Depending on the fare conditions:

- Airport taxes may be refundable.
- Government taxes may be refundable.
- Service fees or cancellation charges may apply.

The final refund amount depends on the ticket rules.

# Flight Cancelled by Emirates

If Emirates cancels a flight or makes a significant schedule change, eligible passengers may receive options such as:

- Rebooking on another flight
- Travel credit where applicable
- Refund according to applicable regulations and fare conditions

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I get a full refund?

Only if the fare conditions allow a full refund or if you qualify under applicable airline or regulatory policies.

---

## How do I request a refund?

If you booked directly with Emirates, submit a refund request through the official refund process. If you booked through a travel agency or another airline, contact the original booking provider.

---

## How long does a refund take?

Refund processing varies depending on the payment method, country of purchase, and banking institution.

---

## Will my refund be returned to my original payment method?

In most eligible cases, approved refunds are issued using the original payment method.

---

## Are cancellation fees refundable?

Cancellation fees are generally not refundable unless otherwise stated in the fare conditions or required by applicable regulations.

---

## Can I request a refund for unused airport taxes?

Unused taxes and government charges may be refundable depending on the ticket conditions and applicable regulations.
