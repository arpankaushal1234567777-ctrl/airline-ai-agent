# Overview

Passengers travelling with pets or oversized items should review Emirates baggage requirements before departure. Certain items require advance approval, additional fees, or special handling.

Acceptance depends on aircraft type, destination regulations, operational requirements, and applicable airline policies.

---

# Travelling With Pets

Pets may be accepted for transport depending on:

- Destination regulations
- Animal type
- Health documentation
- Import and export requirements
- Airline operational policies

Passengers should contact Emirates before booking travel with pets.

---

# Dogs

Dogs may be transported according to Emirates pet travel policies.

Requirements may include:

- Approved travel crate
- Health certificates
- Vaccination records
- Import permits where applicable

Some countries impose quarantine or additional documentation requirements.

---

# Cats

Cats may also be accepted under Emirates pet transportation policies.

Owners should ensure:

- Proper travel crate
- Required vaccinations
- Health certificates
- Destination entry requirements

---

# Service Animals

Service animals may be accepted on eligible routes subject to:

- Destination regulations
- Required documentation
- Airline approval

Passengers travelling with service animals should notify Emirates before travel.

---

# Pet Containers

Pet carriers should:

- Be strong and secure
- Provide sufficient ventilation
- Allow the animal to stand, turn, and lie comfortably
- Meet applicable airline requirements

Improper containers may not be accepted for transport.

---

# Musical Instruments

Passengers may travel with musical instruments.

Small instruments that comply with cabin baggage limits may be carried onboard.

Examples include:

- Violin
- Ukulele
- Flute
- Small guitar

Larger instruments may require:

- Checked baggage
- Purchase of an additional seat
- Special handling

---

# Sports Equipment

Many sporting items may be accepted as checked baggage.

Examples include:

- Golf clubs
- Cricket equipment
- Tennis rackets
- Fishing equipment
- Ski equipment
- Snowboards
- Surfboards
- Diving equipment
- Archery equipment

Oversized items may incur additional baggage charges.

---

# Bicycles

Bicycles may be transported when packed appropriately.

Passengers should:

- Remove pedals where required
- Deflate tyres if instructed
- Protect delicate components
- Use suitable protective packaging

---

# Baby Equipment

Passengers travelling with infants may generally carry baby equipment.

Examples include:

- Strollers
- Pushchairs
- Baby bassinets
- Car seats

Availability and acceptance depend on aircraft type and operational requirements.

---

# Mobility Equipment

Passengers may travel with mobility aids such as:

- Wheelchairs
- Walking aids
- Mobility scooters (subject to battery requirements)

Advance notification is recommended.

---

# Oversized Baggage

Oversized baggage includes items exceeding standard baggage dimensions or weight.

Examples include:

- Large televisions
- Musical instruments
- Sports equipment
- Exhibition materials

Additional charges or special handling may apply.

---

# Valuable Items

Passengers are encouraged to carry valuable items in cabin baggage whenever possible.

Examples include:

- Jewellery
- Cash
- Travel documents
- Electronics
- Laptops
- Cameras

Valuable items should generally not be packed in checked baggage.

---

# Fragile Items

Fragile items should be packed securely.

Examples include:

- Glass items
- Ceramics
- Artwork
- Musical instruments
- Photography equipment

Special handling does not guarantee protection from damage.

---

# Important Notes

- Destination countries may have additional pet import rules.
- Advance approval may be required for certain special baggage.
- Oversized baggage may incur additional charges.
- Some items are subject to aircraft limitations.
- Airline policies may change without prior notice.

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I travel with my dog?

Yes. Dogs may be transported subject to Emirates policies and destination regulations.

---

## Can I travel with my cat?

Yes. Cats may generally be transported when airline and destination requirements are met.

---

## Can I take my pet on the plane?

Pet transportation depends on airline policy, destination regulations, and the type of animal.

---

## Can I travel with a service dog?

Service animals may be accepted on eligible routes with the required documentation.

---

## Can I carry my guitar?

Small guitars may travel in the cabin if they meet baggage limits. Larger instruments may require checked baggage or an additional seat.

---

## Can I carry a violin?

Yes. Small musical instruments are generally permitted if they comply with cabin baggage limits.

---

## Can I carry sports equipment?

Yes. Many sports items are accepted as checked baggage, although oversized equipment may incur additional charges.

---

## Can I carry golf clubs?

Yes. Golf clubs are generally accepted as checked sports equipment.

---

## Can I carry my bicycle?

Yes. Properly packed bicycles may be accepted according to Emirates baggage policies.

---

## Can I carry my surfboard?

Surfboards may generally be accepted as special baggage, subject to size restrictions and applicable fees.

---

## Can I bring a stroller?

Yes. Strollers and pushchairs are generally accepted for passengers travelling with infants.

---

## Can I carry a wheelchair?

Yes. Mobility equipment is generally accepted, and wheelchair assistance may also be requested.

---

## Can I check in my television?

Large televisions may be accepted as oversized baggage depending on packaging, dimensions, and applicable baggage rules.

---

## Should I pack valuables in checked baggage?

No. Valuable items such as laptops, cameras, jewellery, and important documents should generally be carried in cabin baggage whenever possible.

---

## What happens if my baggage is oversized?

Oversized baggage may require special handling and additional baggage charges depending on the size, weight, and route.
